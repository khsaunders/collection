Butters, the computer cat

I like writing code that solves problems for people and creating pleasant user experiences. I value honesty, creativity, and collaboration. My background is in communication and marketing, but I decided that building things for the Internet was my true passion. When I'm not coding, I like drawing and hanging out with my cat, Butters.

I've been a visual artist for over a decade and was fortunate enough to grow up surrounded by computers. My fascination with web design began in the days of Myspace (back in the good ol' days). My friends and I would spend hours customizing our layouts, discovering which paisley or pinstripe background best suited our styles.

My love for design persisted through high school and college (and, fortunately, evolved). I've designed logos for non-profits, created concept art for children's books, and spent time making (and remaking) designs in programs like Illustrator, Affinity, and Sketch for fun.
